Introduction

"Shoot'em up" project made in Unity using C#.

I am unsure what features will be implemented since this is just a small project I am working on in my free time. Hopefully one day it will benefit someone who is learning Unity.

Features

For now, the player can pick up different weapons (pistol, smg, shotgun, machine gun) which use ammunition available in limited quantity. The enemies walk straight towards the player and attack him/her when he/she is in range.
